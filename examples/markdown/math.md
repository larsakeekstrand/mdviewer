# Math (KaTeX)

MDViewer renders math expressions using KaTeX. Use `$…$` for inline and `$$…$$` for display blocks.

## Inline Math

Einstein's mass-energy equivalence: $E = mc^2$.

The quadratic formula gives roots at $x = \dfrac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}$.

Euler's identity is often called the most beautiful equation: $e^{i\pi} + 1 = 0$.

## Display Blocks

The integral of $x^2$ over the unit interval:

$$\int_0^1 x^2\,dx = \tfrac{1}{3}$$

The Gaussian (normal) distribution:

$$f(x) = \frac{1}{\sigma\sqrt{2\pi}}\,e^{-\frac{(x-\mu)^2}{2\sigma^2}}$$
